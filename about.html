About Lake Placid

Design Philosophy

The Lake Placid Group uses the most innovative technology, construction and architecture to ensure customer satisfaction. Customers participate in the design process from the beginning - seeing immediate, detailed results - and ultimately help to create their own dream home.

Customer Oriented

Lake Placid doesn't simply build condominiums - it builds lasting relationships. From interactive design through sound, responsible construction techniques, everything we do is designed to create trust and respect between homebuilder and homeowner. We use state of the art Customer Relations Management software to keep our customers current with our progress.
