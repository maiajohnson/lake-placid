    Chief Executive Officer

He has developed commercial, residential and hotel buildings in Alberta, Saskatchewan and British Colombia. Mr. Lobsinger retired as the CEO of several public companies, with market caps in the hundreds of millions, to concentrate on real estate development. Mr. Lobsinger has a B.A and a B.COMM.
